About Aroma

Aroma was founded in 1998 by Executive Chef, Mario Segale. Prior to Aroma, Chef Segale trained across Europe for more than ten years under masters such as Marco Pierre White and Massimo Bottura.

Chef Segale’s cuisine has been described by food critics as elegant, unpretentious, and true to its Italian roots. Our restaurant specialises in modern Italian cuisine that respects traditional techniques and made with the finest local ingredients.

Chef Segale is a firm believer in the “farm-to-table” concept. Many of our dishes are created with fresh locally-sourced ingredients grown within a 50-kilometer radius of our restaurant.

In recognition of our commitment to quality, Aroma was awarded its first Michelin Star in 2001. In 2004, we were awarded with our second Michelin Star. Our restaurant has held its two Michelin Star status since 2004.
